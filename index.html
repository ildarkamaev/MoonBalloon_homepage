The future is now, space is here.

What is MoonBalloon?

Have you ever thought of exploring earth and space at the same time? Now it is possible!

MoonBalloon will take you higher than you have ever hoped for. We offer you to be one of the first people to experience totally private space tourism with fully customizable MoonPod. Your journey to stratosphere can be wild with your best friends, romantic with your loved one or poetic and peaceful on your own. Our MoonPod will take you up to 30 kilometres with the people and decorations of your own choosing. There is only one question left to ask. “Are you ready to experience Earth from up high in the most comfortable way possible?”.

Young and innovative startup MoonBalloon is going to make your trip to outer space the most enjoyable event of your lifetime. We’re providing you with affordable MoonPod, that will take you up to 30 kilometres, where you can see Earth from totally new perspective.

How this works

From home to the skies

The magical MoonBalloon journey will start from your home town with a private jet from our behalf and the trip to the edge of the space with MoonBalloon will last for several hours. Location of takeoff will be announced a week before your tour to the edge of space and afterwards you’re going to have a smooth landing in a larger reservoir, where our experienced recovery team will provide you the most comfortable trip to the shore with our very own luxury yacht. The destination is yours to choose.

To the Moon and back again

MoonBalloon tech consists of a MoonPod and a huge balloon, that will bring you to the stratosphere and afterwards down to earth. The reinforced balloon is filled with hydrogen that is one of the most reliable and eco-friendly substances there is. The worst case scenario would be, that hydrogen would find a way out of the balloon and your trip to stratosphere would be few minutes shorter. But the reinforced balloon is tested countless times and you won’t have to worry about it ruining your experience.

Top Quality Gear

The MoonPod itself is furnished with top quality gear, that helps you relish the whole journey. You won’t have to worry about navigation, safety or communication - we already have thought about everything for you. The MoonPod is furnished with a high-end comfortable bed, an exquisite plate with tasty delicacies and a bottle of real bubbly champagne straight from Champagne, France. It also has a WiFi connection for you to share your experience instantly with your friends. The MoonPod also has a lavatory with sleek design, so you won’t have to worry about your personal hygiene.

Cost

The aim of MoonBalloon team is to bring the space to you with the most comfortable way possible, so that you could enjoy every second from your journey to space. Depending on the duration of the journey, your travel costs will be around 70 000€ per person. This project got it’s kickstart from Garage48 Spacetech 2019 and it’s been active ever since.
