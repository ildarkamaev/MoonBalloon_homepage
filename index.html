What is MoonBalloon?

Have you ever thought of exploring earth and space at the same time? Now it is possible!

MoonBalloon will take you higher than you have ever hoped for. We offer you to be one of the first people to experience totally private space tourism with fully customizable MoonPod. Your journey to stratosphere can be wild with your best friends, romantic with your loved one or poetic and peaceful on your own. Our MoonPod will take you up to 30 kilometres with the people and decorations of your own choosing. There is only one question left to ask. “Are you ready to experience Earth from up high in the most comfortable way possible?”.
